Electra iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak Not Working - Troubleshooting Guide

Jailbreak iOS & tvOS Tutorials

If Electra Jailbreak for iOS 11.3.1 is not working then try these tips to fix the problem. 

Common issues that currently exist with the iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak:

  • iOS device (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch) keeps rebooting when tapping the Jailbreak button, but it does not install Cydia
  • rootfs remount
  • Error: exploit
  • Error:amfid patch

Electra iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak Not Working - Troubleshooting Guide:

Follow ALL these steps before you try to Jailbreak your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch:

1. Enable Airplane mode

Access the Control Center, and tap on Airplane toggle to enable it.

2. Turn off WiFi

(if Airplane mode didn't already take care of turning it off).

3. Turn Off Siri

Launch the Settings app and navigate to Siri and Search, and tap on Listen for “Hey Siri” and Press Home button for Siri or Press Side Button for Siri toggle and tap Turn Off Siri.

4. Delete iOS Software Update

To delete the iOS Software Update, launch the Settings app and navigate to General > iPhone Storage > iOS 11.4 (or any other iOS software update you see in the list), tap on it, then tap Delete Update, and then Delete Update in the confirmation popup to delete the iOS software update.

5. Disable Passcode

To disable the passcode, launch the Settings app, and tap on Face ID and Passcode or Touch ID & Passcode, enter the Passcode, then tap Turn Off Passcode to disable it.


6. Disable Find My iPhone

To turn off Find My iPhone, navigate to Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Find My iPhone, then tap on the Find My iPhone toggle. Enter the Apple ID and password when prompted to turn it off.

7. Reboot your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch:

Make sure that after the Reboot everything mentioned above is still disabled and Airplane mode is still enabled.

8. Jailbreak your iOS Device:

Try to Jailbreak your iOS device (iPhone, iPad, iPod touch) again and (if it still fails) repeat the Jailbreaking Process for at least 10 Times. So launch the Electra app and keep trying until it works. If the jailbreak is successful, you should see the Cydia Icon on the Home screen.

If Cydia Keeps Crashing, your device is still not jailbroken, launch the Electra app, and tap on "Enable Jailbreak", to jailbreak your iOS device. Try this multiple times.

Hard Reset

If your device suddenly displays a black screen with a spinner on it after Electra’s UI has reached Stage 2/3, the jailbreak has succeeded. You should hard reset your iPhone, iPad or iPod touch to see Cydia on the Home screen.

You can enable Find My iPhone, Passcode, disable Airplane mode again after the jailbreak of iPhone, iPad or iPod touch.

If these tips won't fix your jailbreaking issue, keep up to date with new releases of the Electra Jailbreak Tool, the Electra Team continously releases new versions with bug fixes (i.e. Electra v1.0, v1.0.1, v1.02).
