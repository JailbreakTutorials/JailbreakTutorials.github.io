[Jailbreak News] iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak 1.0.1 Released - [Electra]

Jailbreak iOS & tvOS Tutorials

Shortly after the iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak release, the Electra Jailbreak Developer Team released version 1.0.1 of the Electra Jailbreak for iOS 11.3.1 with some bug fixes.

Electra Jailbreak Tool v1.0.1 changelog:

  1. Fix app icon not showing up for certain users
  2. Fix nonce Generator not being set certain times
