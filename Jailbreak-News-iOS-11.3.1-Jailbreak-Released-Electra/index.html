[Jailbreak News] iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak is Released! - [Electra]

Jailbreak iOS & tvOS Tutorials

The iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak has been released!

After a delay, because of stability issues (reasons for the delay of the Electra iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak release), it seems that the iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak is now stable enough for a public release.

The Electra Jailbreak Developer Team released two Versions of the Electra Jailbreak Tool, one Version for users with an Apple Developer Account and one Version for users without an Apple Developer Account. The version which requires an Apple Developer Account is suppose to give a higher success rate for accomplishing a successfull iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak. But you might need several attempts no matter which version of the iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak Tool you pick.
