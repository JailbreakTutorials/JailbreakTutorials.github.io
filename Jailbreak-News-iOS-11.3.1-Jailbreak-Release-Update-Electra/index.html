[Jailbreak News] iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak Release Update - [Electra]

It was just recently announced that the iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak will be released soon.

The reason for the delay of the iOS 11.3.1 Electra Jailbreak realease soon, is because of some code instabilities. Coolstar, the Jailbreak developer behind the Electra Jailbreak, is waiting on Spark Zheng, iOS Hacker and engineer, to provide a more stable solution.

Zheng has asked to be patient. The Electra iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak will also be a semi-tethered jailbreak like Electra iOS 11.1.2 jailbreak. A semi-untethered jailbreak is similar to an untethered jailbreak, it gives you the ability to reboot your iOS device on its own. On each boot, the iOS device startup sequence is unmodified and it boots into its original, non-jailbroken state. However, instead of needing to use a tool from a computer to re-jailbreak the iOS device again, (like a tethered Jailbreak) the user can re-jailbreak their device with the help of the Electra app, already running on the iOS device.

The iOS 11.3.1 Jailbreak with Electra will support all iOS 11 versions except for iOS 11.4 and iOS 11.4.1. So it will support iOS 11.2, iOS 11.2.1, iOS 11.2.5, iOS 11.2.6, iOS 11.3, iOS 11.3.1 and also iOS 11 – iOS 11.1.2.

At the same time there is already a Jailbreak for iOS 11.4 Beta and iOS 12 Beta in development.
