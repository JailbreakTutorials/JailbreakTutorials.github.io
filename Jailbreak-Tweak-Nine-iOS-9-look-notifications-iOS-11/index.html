[Jailbreak Tweak] Nine: iOS 9 look for notifications on iOS 11

Jailbreak iOS & tvOS Tutorials

Jailbreak Tweak Nine makes your incoming iOS 11 notifications look more like iOS 9 notifications than.

This includes also the banners you’d see while receiving notifications on the Home screen or inside of apps.

Nine adds a preference pane to the Settings app where you can configure a few options:

Jailbreak Tweak Nine settings options:

  • Toggle the tweak on or off on demand
  • Enable iPhone X-like banners
  • Toggle header backgrounds on or off
  • Disable SpringBoard icons
  • Force display the Lock screen wallpaper
  • Configure a custom blue radius for the transparent background
  • Choose how many notifications can display at one time
  • Configure darkening level for underlying notifications
  • Configure darkening level for historic notifications

There is also a Respring button at the top right of the preference pane, which should be used after changing the Jailbreak Tweak’s settings.

Jailbreak Tweak Nine is available for free from Cydia’s BigBoss repository. It’s certified to work on jailbroken iOS 11 devices.
