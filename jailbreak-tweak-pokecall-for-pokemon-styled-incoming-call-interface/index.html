[Jailbreak Tweak] PokeCall for Pokémon styled incoming call interface

Jailbreak iOS & tvOS Tutorials

The new Jailbreak Tweak PokeCall by iOS developer Frozen Penguin changes your iPhone’s incoming call interface to a look inspired by the classic Pokémon games.

This is of how the Jailbreak Tweak PokeCall replaces the stock incoming call interface in iOS with a Pokémon look and feel.

Cydia description:

  • Fight – Answers the call
  • Run – Declines the call
  • Hide – Minimizes the call screen to a non-intrusive call banner
  • Switch – Restores the stock call screen

Pokémon styled call banner:

You can configure the Jailbreak Tweak PokeCall from the preference pane that gets added to your Settings app:

Jailbreak Tweak PokeCall Settings:

  • Toggle the Jailbreak Tweak on or off on demand
  • Switch between Gold/Silver/Crystal game interfaces
  • Choose a Pokémon to represent yourself (1st or 2nd generation)
  • Choose a Pokémon to represent your contact(s)
  • Configure Pokémon-centric ringtone(s) to match the aesthetic
  • And more…

The Jailbreak Tweak PokeCall works with phone calls from the stock Phone app and FaceTime app, as well as calls from third-party apps such as WhatsApp.

The Jailbreak Tweak is available for $2.00 from Cydia’s Packix repository and works on all jailbroken iOS 9, 10, and 11 devices.

Note: The developer is working on a fix for iOS 10 devices and is working on an update as of version 1.0.7.

Obviously, in order yout install the PokeCall Jailbreak Tweak,you have to jailbreak your iPhone first.
